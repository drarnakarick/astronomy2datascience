Astrophysics to Data Science

Visualising the career paths of astronomers who moved from academia to data science

The Science to Data Science (S2DS) and Insight Data Science are intensive post-doctoral training fellowships that bridge the gap between academia and data science in industry. The aim of this project was to explore how and when do astronomers move into data science. How many do postdocs first? How many go through a data science fellowship programs? How many move into data science without a data science fellowship?

The data comes from the LinkedIn profiles of 116 astronomers who moved from astronomy to data science at some point in their career.
